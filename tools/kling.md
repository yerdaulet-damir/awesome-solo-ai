# Kling AI — high-fidelity long-duration video

> Realistic motion and physics, longer clips than most models.

## What is Kling AI?

**Kling is an AI video generator known for high-fidelity, longer-duration clips with strong motion realism and physical consistency.** It generates from text or image and is a top pick when output quality and clip length matter more than raw speed, available via app and API.

## Quick facts

- **Website:** [klingai.com](https://klingai.com)
- **API:** yes — generation API (direct and via hosts like fal)
- **CLI:** none official; API-driven
- **MCP:** no official server; call via the API or a wrapper
- **Free tier:** limited daily credits

## What you build with it

- Longer cinematic sequences for shorts and ads.
- Realistic image-to-video animation.
- Hero clips where motion quality is the differentiator.

## Example

> "Generate an 8-second clip of waves at sunset, realistic motion, image-to-video from this still."
