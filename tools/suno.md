# Suno — text-to-song with vocals

> Describe a song and get a full track — vocals, instruments, structure.

## What is Suno?

**Suno is an AI music generator that turns a text prompt into a complete song, including vocals, instrumentation, and arrangement.** It's the leading text-to-music tool for creators who need original, royalty-considered audio — intros, background tracks, or full songs — without a studio.

## Quick facts

- **Website:** [suno.com](https://suno.com)
- **API:** yes — via managed/third-party providers
- **CLI:** none official
- **MCP:** no official server; reachable through managed API wrappers
- **Free tier:** yes — limited free generations

## What you build with it

- Background music and intros for [video content](../playbooks/faceless-content-factory.md).
- Original songs and jingles for a brand.
- Theme music for a podcast or channel.

## Example

> "Make a 60-second upbeat lo-fi track with soft vocals for a product intro."

## Note

The AI-music space is in active licensing transition — check current terms before commercial use. [Stable Audio](https://stableaudio.com) offers a commercially-clean, licensed-dataset alternative.
