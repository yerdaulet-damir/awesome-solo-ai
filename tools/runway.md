# Runway — industry-standard AI video

> Text and image to video with fine motion control — the studio default for AI video.

## What is Runway?

**Runway is an AI video generation platform for creating and editing video from text or images, with precise motion and camera control.** Its Gen-4 family is a default for creators and studios producing ads, B-roll, and short-form, available through the app and an API.

## Quick facts

- **Website:** [runwayml.com](https://runwayml.com)
- **API:** yes — developer API for generation
- **CLI:** none official; API-driven
- **MCP:** no official server; call via the API or a thin wrapper
- **Free tier:** limited trial credits

## What you build with it

- B-roll and cinematic shots for [content](../playbooks/faceless-content-factory.md).
- Ad creatives and product videos from a prompt or still.
- Image-to-video animation of brand assets.

## Example

> "Animate this product still with a slow dolly-in, 4 seconds, cinematic."
