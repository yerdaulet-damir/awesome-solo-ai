# HeyGen — scripted avatar video

> Turn a script into a talking-head video with your AI avatar — no camera, no studio.

## What is HeyGen?

**HeyGen is an AI avatar platform that generates spokesperson and talking-head videos from text.** You clone yourself once, then produce videos from scripts via the dashboard or API, including voice cloning and translation. It ships an official MCP server, so an agent can drive the whole "script → voiced avatar clip" flow.

## Quick facts

- **Website:** [heygen.com](https://heygen.com)
- **API:** yes — video, avatar, and voice endpoints
- **CLI:** none official; API-driven
- **MCP:** yes, official → [HeyGen MCP page](../mcp/heygen.md)
- **Free tier:** yes — limited monthly credits

## What you build with it

- A faceless or personal-brand video channel from scripts (see [Faceless Content Factory](../playbooks/faceless-content-factory.md)).
- Localized versions of one video across many languages.
- Personalized outbound or onboarding videos at scale.

## Example

With the [MCP](../mcp/heygen.md) connected:

> "Render my avatar saying this 30-second script and download the mp4."
